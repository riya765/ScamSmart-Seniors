Welcome to ScamSmart Seniors

In today’s digital world, scams targeting seniors are increasingly prevalent and sophisticated. At ScamSmart Seniors, we are dedicated to empowering older adults with the knowledge and tools necessary to recognize, avoid, and report cyber scams. Our mission is to create a safe online environment where seniors can navigate the digital landscape with confidence.

As many seniors may be unaware of the tactics used by scammers, it is crucial to stay informed about common scams that specifically target older adults. From phishing emails to fraudulent phone calls, understanding these threats is the first step in protecting yourself and your loved ones.
